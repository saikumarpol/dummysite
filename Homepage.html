FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions & Answer

Q1. What We Can Offer ?
We have well trained intelligent advisors who are here to answer all your queries, calm your doubts and be your guide through this entire process. With versatile knowledge and understanding they can help you find the courses and resources you want.

Many of our team are also top university graduates so they can relate to the position you are in and their aim is to help get you to the school, college or university that is best suited to you and your needs.
Q2. What is Compliance Assistance ?
For potential international students, the process of coming to study in another country may seem daunting, but our team is here to make this a swift, easy transition.

We have a team that is dedicated to helping with the visa process and are available to answer any questions relating to this and help students to apply and arrange any visa paperwork or applications.
Q3. What are our Impeccable Admissions Services ?
Available for UK, EU and International student ( with some priority for areas in South Asia and the MENA region) we have an exceptional admissions service to ensure that we get you in contact with the top universities in the best time frame, to ensure your rate of success is higher with efficient contact and aid.
Q4. How do we Follow up ?
At SBUB Group the dedication to the service we provide means that we want to ensure that everything goes how it is expected, so we enjoy keeping up with our students and checking on their progress even once they have successfully made it to the university or school they wanted.

Your wellbeing and success mean huge amounts to us as the goal is the get you the qualifications to go on and triumph in your chosen career.

We start the process with you and will be there to support you until the end.
